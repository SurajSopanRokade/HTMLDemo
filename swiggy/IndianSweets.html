Indian Sweets Delight

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian sweet made from khoya and soaked in sugar syrup. These soft, golden-brown dumplings are often enjoyed during festivals and special occasions.

Rasgulla

Rasgulla, originating from West Bengal, is a spongy and delectable sweet made from chhena and soaked in sugar syrup. It's a favorite during celebrations and festivals.
